Stir Fried Bok Choy

Stir Frying Greens

Stir frying greens is a fast and easy way to cook a delicious side to any meal. It pairs great with rice dishes, follow along using your favorite leafy green. For this recipe I will be using Bok Choy. There are mny kinds of Bok Choy, they are all delicious and can all be cooked following this recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1lb Bok Choy
  • 2-3 tbsp neutral oil
  • salt

Steps

  1. Separate and wash
    • Separate the leaves of the Bok Choy.
    • Wash using a colander.
    • Pat dry with papr towels. (otherwise the oil will splatter when you put it in the pan)
  2. Stir Fry
    • Heat 2-3 tbsp oil in a wok over medium-high heat.
    • Add bok choy and stir fry for 3-5 minutes.
  3. salt
    • Add salt to taste(wait until the very end to add salt or your greens wil let out too much water and will steam instad of stir fry)

Enjoy!
